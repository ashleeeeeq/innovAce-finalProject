About Us

The Petalgram Flowershop

Petalgram Flowershop has been blooming in Dangwa, Laong Laan, Manila for more than two decades, under the ownership of Laila H. Quiban. Over the years, we’ve become a trusted source for beautiful flowers and floral arrangements for all occasions.

We believe in the power of fresh blooms to brighten someone’s day, and we take pride in using only the highest quality flowers. Our friendly and experienced team is dedicated to providing exceptional service, ensuring your floral experience with Petalgram is a delightful one.

Let Petalgram Flowershop help you tell your story with flowers. Visit us or browse our selection online to find the perfect arrangement for your needs.
